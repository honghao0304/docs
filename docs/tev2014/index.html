The 9th workshop of TeV physics working Group will be held on May 15-18, 2014 at the South Campus of Sun Yat-sen (Zhongshan) University, Guangzhou, China.

Topics to be covered:

  • The progress and new results from the experiments at the LHC and the plans for the 14 TeV collision;
  • Physics of Higgs boson;
  • Phenomenology of new physics;
  • Studies of CEPC+SppS;
  • B physics on the hadron colliders;
  • Other related physics.
