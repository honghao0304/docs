Transportation

October 8, 2013


Transportation to South Campus of Sun Yat-sen University

a. If you take a flight to Baiyun International Airport in Guangzhou, you can:

　　1. Take a taxi to SYSU (about RMB150). The drive takes about one hour.

　　2. Take the Airport Express Bus and continue with a taxi: Take the Airport Express Bus (about RMB30) to the New Pearl River Hotel (line 6) or to Guangzhou Textile Exchange Park (line 10), get off and take a taxi (RMB10) to the South Campus.

　　3. Take the metro at Airport South Station to SYSU (about RMB 12): Please take Line 3 (Airport S. to Tiyuxi Road) to the Tiyuxi Road Station. Transfer to another Line 3 (Tianhe Coach Terminal to Panyu Square) and when you get to the Kecun Station, then you can transfer to Line 8 (towards Fenghuang Xincun). Get off the train at Sun Yat-sen University Station (Exit A).

Taxi service is very convenient in Guangzhou. The fee is charged by distance. An available Taxi displays a red sign of "空车(Empty)" near the front windshield. Please keep the printed invoice when you get off so that you can contact the taxi driver or the taxi company in case of any problems.

b. If you take a flight to Hong Kong, you can:

　　1. Take another flight from Hong Kong to Baiyun International Airport in Guangzhou, then follow the above instruction.

　　2. Take the HK-Guangzhou Express (three steps as follows):
　　　(1). Take a taxi from HK Airport to Hung Hom (红|) Train Station (about HKD350).
　　　(2). Get on the HK-Guangzhou Express and you will arrive at the East Train Station of Guangzhou. (about HKD190-230)
　　　(3). Follow the instruction there and you will find the metro entry. (South Campus: line 3+line 8). Or you can take a taxi from the East Train Station of Guangzhou to the South Campus of SYSU (about RMB 50), you may tell taxi driver that your destination is "广州市海珠区新港西路135号中山大学" in Chinese.

　　3. Take a bus to Guangzhou (about HKD230-250): Ask the Bus Service Staff to check bus arrangements heading for Guangzhou.

c. Some informtion about getting from Hong Kong airport to Guangzhou suggested by Prof. Ming-chung Chu:

　　1. Direct train

　　- go from the airport to Hung Hom station: take the airport express train and change at Tsing Yi Station. The trip takes about 38 minutes. The MTR map and information is here: http://www.mtr.com.hk/jplanner/flash_eng/index.php?sid=47&eid=64&x=23&y=4

　　- then take the direct train from Hung Hom station to Guangzhou.

You can find out the schedule and fare of the train from Hung Hom station to Guangzhou here: http://www.it3.mtr.com.hk/b2c/frmIndex.asp?strLang=Eng

Basically there is a train evey hour, and the trip from HK to Guangzhou East is about 2 hours. The one way ticket is about HK$190, and you can buy the ticket on-line.

　　2. There is an airport bus from the airport to Guangzhu. You just have to walk to Terminal 2 where they have a coach station for buses going to mainland. However, I cannot find any English information from the website of the bus company: http://ctsbus.hkcts.com/routes/air/air-index.html

It could be a more convenient way than the direct train, but I don't know where they stop in Guangzhou.
