Projects for undergraduate students

These projects are provided for undergraduate students at School of Physics of Sun Yat-Sen University.

Dark matter, particle physics and cosmology

    Project 1: Dark matter (guided by Zhao-Huan Yu and Hong-Hao Zhang)
  • Kolb, Turner, The Early Universe pdf (Chapter 2, 3, 5)
  • Dodelson, Modern Cosmology pdf (Chapter 1-4)

 

Field theory and mathematical physics

    Project 1: Conformal field theory (guided by Yiwen Pan and Hong-Hao Zhang)
  • Francesco, Mathieu, Senechal, Conformal Field Theory pdf (Particularly interesting chapters are Chapter 1-7, 10)
  • Ginsparg, Applied Conformal Field Theory pdf, hep-th/9108028
  • Tong, String Theory pdf, 0908.0333 (Chapter 4)

 

    Project 2: Supersymmetric theory (guided by Yiwen Pan and Hong-Hao Zhang)
  • Hori, Katz, Klemm, Pandharipande, Thomas, Vafa, Vakil, Zaslow, Mirror Symmetry pdf (Chapter 9-16)
  • Bilal, Introduction to Supersymmetry pdf, hep-th/0101055
  • Rocek, Introduction to Supersymmetry pdf
